Kitchen Cabinetry

The kitchen is the heart of your home, and we treat it that way. California Cabinets offers a full spectrum of kitchen cabinet options — from semi-custom lines with fast lead times to completely bespoke, fully custom configurations that make the most of every inch of your space.

We carry Diamond Cabinets and other premium lines, giving you access to hundreds of door styles, finishes, glazes, and storage solutions. Our designers work with you to create a layout that maximizes functionality while achieving a look that's unmistakably yours.

  • Custom and semi-custom cabinet lines
  • Hundreds of door styles and finish options
  • 3D design renderings before ordering
  • Full range of storage accessories & organizers
  • Island and peninsula configurations
  • Crown molding, glass inserts, open shelving
  • Turn-key design and installation

Bath Cabinetry

Your bathroom should be a sanctuary — a place of calm and luxury that you look forward to every morning. California Cabinets brings the same passion and precision to bath design that we bring to kitchens.

We offer a comprehensive range of vanity styles, floating cabinets, linen towers, and medicine storage solutions designed to transform your bathroom into a spa-like retreat while maximizing storage and utility.

  • Freestanding and floating vanities
  • Double and single vanity configurations
  • Linen towers and built-in storage

  • Wide finish selection including matte, gloss, and wood-look

Service 03

Outdoor Living & Kitchens

California's outdoor lifestyle deserves an outdoor kitchen that's as well-designed as your interior spaces. As an authorized NatureKast dealer, California Cabinets brings resort-quality, weatherproof cabinetry to your backyard.

NatureKast's High Density Resin (HDR) construction is completely weatherproof — no warping, no fading, no rust — making it the premier choice for outdoor kitchens in the Central Valley's heat. Our outdoor specialists will design a complete outdoor kitchen, bar, or entertainment space that fits your lifestyle and lot.

  • Authorized NatureKast dealer and certified installer
  • 100% weatherproof HDR cabinet construction
  • Custom outdoor kitchen and bar layouts
  • Grill station, refrigerator, and sink configurations
  • Coordinated countertop and finish selections
  • Design showroom display available to view in person
