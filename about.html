Who We Are

More Than Just Cabinets

California Cabinets was founded on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a kitchen and living space that truly reflects who they are. We're not just a cabinet supplier — we're your design partner from the first conversation to the final installation.

Based in Fresno, we've spent years building relationships with the finest cabinet manufacturers in the country and honing our craft to serve families across the Central Valley. Our showroom on W. Bedford Ave brings it all to life — with hundreds of samples, real vignettes, and a team that genuinely loves what they do.

Whether you're tackling a simple kitchen refresh or a full-home renovation, we bring the same care, creativity, and commitment to excellence to every project.

Meet the Owner

Ken Brown

Ken Brown has been in the cabinet and home design industry for over two decades, developing a deep passion for the intersection of functional design and lasting beauty. He founded California Cabinets with a mission to bring big-city design expertise to the Fresno community.

Ken works closely with every client — listening to their needs, understanding their lifestyle, and translating those insights into kitchens and spaces that feel uniquely theirs. His hands-on approach and encyclopedic knowledge of cabinet construction, finishes, and layout make him an invaluable partner in any renovation.

When Ken isn't in the showroom, you'll find him visiting job sites, attending industry shows, and staying ahead of the latest design trends so his clients always get the best.

How It Works

Our Simple 4-Step Process

From your first call to the final reveal, we make the design and installation process easy, transparent, and enjoyable.

1

Free Consultation

Visit our Fresno showroom or call us to discuss your project. We'll listen to your goals, timeline, and budget — no pressure, just great conversation.

2

Design & Selection

Our designers create a custom layout and walk you through cabinet styles, finishes, and hardware. We use 3D rendering so you can see your kitchen before it's built.

3

Order & Delivery

Once you've approved the design, we place the order, track production, and schedule delivery. We keep you updated every step of the way.

4

Professional Installation

Our experienced installation team handles every detail for a flawless finish. We don't leave until you're completely thrilled with the result.
