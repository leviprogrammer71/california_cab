Common Questions

What People Ask Us

Do you offer free consultations? +

Yes, absolutely. All consultations are complimentary and there's never any pressure to buy. Come visit our showroom, browse samples, and let's talk about your project — no strings attached.

How long does a typical cabinet project take? +

Lead times vary depending on the product line and scope of your project. Semi-custom lines typically have 3–6 week lead times, while fully custom orders may take 8–14 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you handle installation? +

Yes — we offer full turn-key service including design, ordering, delivery, and professional installation. You work with one team from start to finish, which means better communication and a more cohesive result.

Can I see a 3D rendering before I commit? +

Yes! We use professional design software to create 3D renderings of your kitchen or bath before any order is placed. You'll see exactly what the finished product will look like — including door styles, finishes, and layout.

What areas do you serve? +

We primarily serve Fresno, Clovis, Madera, and the surrounding Central Valley communities. If you're unsure whether we serve your area, just give us a call at (559) 291-2678.
